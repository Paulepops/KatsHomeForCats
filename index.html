Services

One owner at a time

I will provide exclusive cat-minding services for one owner at a time and I will care for up to 3 cats for that owner. Check my availability

Reasonable cats

If your cat isn't fussy, the cost of cat-minding will be $25 per cat per day and I will provide their food.

Fussy cats

If your cat is a fussy eater, the cost of cat-minding will be $22 per cat per day and the owner will have to provide their food.

All cats must be fully vaccinated

Cats are generally vaccinated at 8 weeks, 12 weeks, 16 weeks and then once a year, to protect them from diseases such as feline enteritis, cat flu, feline chlamydia and feline immunodeficiency virus (FIV), which can be very serious and even fatal, particularly for kittens. For more information, read the RSPCA information on Vaccinating your pet.

About

I have now retired after 40 years working in local, state and federal government, and the private sector. I am interest in animal welfare, bric-a-brac, family history and lawn bowls.

I have a special room setup for the care and keeping of cats and enjoy the love and attention cats bring to my life.

In 1998 I was part of the Hobart City Council team that won the Australian Animal Management Officers Team of the Year award.
